The classical view of the doge shows him kneeling in front of the Virgin, as well as Saint Mark, here represented with a feather and a lion; sometimes it's just a lion. The attitude is always that of a servant; from early on the attempts to concentrate power by the doge were thwarted, and the whole legislative system was built around that intention.
